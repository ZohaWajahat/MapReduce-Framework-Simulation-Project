Red, vibrant and bold, symbolizes passion and energy. Blue, calm and serene, is often associated with the sky and the sea. Green, fresh and lively, represents growth and renewal. Yellow, bright and cheerful, evokes feelings of happiness and warmth. Orange, a mix of red and yellow, is energetic and attention-grabbing. Purple, regal and mysterious, has long been associated with royalty and creativity. White, pure and simple, stands for peace and cleanliness. Black, deep and powerful, signifies elegance and mystery. Pink, soft and playful, is often linked to love and kindness. Brown, earthy and warm, represents stability and reliability. Gray, neutral and balanced, conveys sophistication and practicality. Each color has its unique emotional and cultural significance, enriching human expression and design.
